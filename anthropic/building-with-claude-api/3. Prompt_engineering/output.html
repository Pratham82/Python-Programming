# Nutrition Plan for Marathon Training

## Daily Caloric Intake: 3000-3500 calories

### Macronutrient Breakdown
- **Carbohydrates**: 55-60% (410-525g) - fuel for endurance
- **Protein**: 15-20% (112-175g) - muscle recovery
- **Fats**: 20-25% (67-97g) - sustained energy

---

## Sample Daily Meal Plan

### Breakfast (700 cal)
- Oatmeal with banana, berries, honey
- 2 whole grain toast with almond butter
- Orange juice + water

### Mid-Morning Snack (300 cal)
- Energy bar or trail mix
- Sports drink or electrolyte beverage

### Lunch (800 cal)
- Grilled chicken breast (150g)
- Brown rice or sweet potato
- Vegetables (broccoli, carrots)
- Olive oil dressing

### Pre-Workout Snack (300 cal)
- Banana + dates
- Rice cakes with jam

### Dinner (700 cal)
- Lean protein (salmon/beef)
- Whole grain pasta
- Mixed vegetables
- Olive oil

### Evening Snack (200 cal)
- Greek yogurt with granola

- Sports drinks during long runs (>90 min)
- Electrolyte replacement post-workout

- Consume carbs + protein within 30 min post-workout
